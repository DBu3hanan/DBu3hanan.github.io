Jamaican Politics in a Nutshell


The bickering and fighting of the Jamaica Labour Party and the People's National Party reminds me of a very common scenario of an abusive and dysfunctional household where two parents constantly fight in front of their children. It is common knowledge that the only ones that are truly affected and left with the pieces to pick up are the children (the citizens of the country).  

There is more focus on spiting each other than working together to achieve a greater good in the country. The parties refuse to put aside party colours and are thus constantly at odds at the unfortunate expense of the regular Jamaican
