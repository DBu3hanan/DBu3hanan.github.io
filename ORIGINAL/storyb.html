Theme of the Website:

The Theme of the website is a black background colour with Gold and White highlights and text.

The text or content on the page were engineered in such a way to imitate movie credits when the website is scrolled.

The movie credit aspect to the page was implemented because of my adoration for movies

The pages are also engineering in such a way that the user cannot see the content of the website without scrolling down

The only thing that is seen is the introductory photo on the page.

When completely scrolled to the buttom of the page the user should also be unable to see the introductory photo

I believe that with the unique way the website is engineered, it gives the website a nice look and feel

It also gives the user a different experience as opposed to other websites

I did not want my website to follow the normal conventions of what a website should look and feel like

Therefore I implemented it in a different way, to my liking of course.
